# Fatima: The Three Quls Every Night

**Persona:** Fatima (Revert)  
**Type:** Islamic Education  
**Fact-check:** Bukhari 5017, 5016, 5015, Muslim 814

---

## Master Content

**HOOK:** The Prophet ﷺ never slept without reciting these three surahs.

**BODY:**
Every night, the Prophet ﷺ cupped his hands, blew into them, and recited Surah Al-Ikhlas, Al-Falaq, and An-Nas. Then he wiped his hands over his body—head, face, front. Three times. (Bukhari 5017). Even during his final illness, Aisha رضي الله عنها did this for him. These Three Quls are a comprehensive spiritual shield: tawheed, protection from external evil, and protection from internal whispers.
