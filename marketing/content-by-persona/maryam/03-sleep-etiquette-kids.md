## Master Content

**HOOK:** 10 rules of sleeping in Islam — teach your kids the adab the Prophet ﷺ followed.

**BODY:**
Islamic sleep etiquette includes: wudu, dusting the bed, sleeping on the right side (not the stomach), reciting adhkar, sleeping early after Isha, and saying the bedtime dua. The Prophet ﷺ disliked sleeping on the stomach (Abu Dawud 5040) and conversation after Isha (Bukhari 568). Each rule in SunnahSleep links to its Hadith source.
